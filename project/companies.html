OpenAI

OpenAI, valued at $86 billion, is a model maker that collaborates with organizations like Morgan Stanley and the government of Iceland. Their AI advancements span natural language understanding, chatbots, and creative content generation.

Microsoft

Microsoft, a tech giant, invests heavily in AI research and development. Their collaboration with OpenAI and integration of AI across products like Azure and Office exemplify their commitment to advancing AI technologies.

Google

Google, valued at $1.6 trillion, pioneers AI through Google. From search algorithms to self-driving cars, Google’s AI innovations impact daily life, making it a key player in the field. Gemini is its developed model, and it is getting more users each day.

Meta

Meta, valued at $1.6 trillion, is a trailblazer in AI innovation. Its Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) lab, led by AI pioneer Yann LeCun, released the Llama family of large language models as open source. These models have been downloaded over 30 million times, empowering developers worldwide to build their own AI solutions without relying on commercial APIs. Meta’s commitment to open sourcing AI technology fosters collaboration and accessibility, making it a driving force in the AI ecosystem12.
