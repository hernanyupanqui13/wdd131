Site Plan - AI Central

Site Name

Artificial Intelligence Central. The reason for this name is to provide a central hub for all things AI. The site will provide information on AI programming, resources, and tutorials.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information and resources on artificial intelligence (AI) programming. The main areas of focus will be on:

  • AI models
  • What is AI and why is it important?
  • AI Companies

Scenarios

The site will be used by developers, students, and anyone interested in learning more about artificial intelligence. They will come to the website with some of these questions in mind:

  • What is Artificial Intelligence?
  • Why is AI important?
  • What types of artificial intelligence exist?
